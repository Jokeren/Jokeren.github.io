Research

My major research area is parallel and distributed algorithms. I focus on developing efficient concurrent data structures, optimizing parallel algorithms on modern architectures. The cornerstone of my future research is the way to make full use of computing resources by combing algorithms and architectures. I also participate in data mining contests as a hobby.
